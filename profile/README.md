# OC Open Consulting SB Srl

[**OC Open Consulting SB Srl**](https://www.oc-group.eu) is an Italian Benefit Corporation with over 15 years of experience in the e-learning domain.

We support organizations in designing, building, and evolving high-quality digital learning ecosystems, based on a clear belief:  
**knowledge is value, and competence is the strongest brand.**

## What We Do

We specialize in the design and implementation of **institutional and corporate online academies**, leveraging robust and widely adopted **open-source Learning Management Systems**, including:

- **ILIAS LMS**
- **Moodle**

Our work combines technical expertise, instructional design, and long-term strategic vision to deliver scalable and sustainable e-learning solutions.

## Our Services

We provide end-to-end e-learning solutions, including:

- LMS analysis, setup, customization, and maintenance  
- Development of custom plugins and integrations  
- Design and production of digital learning content  
- Creation of online courses with **videos, animations, and interactive elements**  
- Production of **video tutorials**, **interactive videos**, and complete **video academies** built around your organizational know-how  

## Why Open Source

We strongly believe that **open source is a strategic choice**, not just a technical one.

Open-source platforms enable transparency, interoperability, and long-term sustainability. They reduce vendor lock-in, foster collaboration, and allow organizations to retain full ownership of their data, processes, and knowledge.

By working with open technologies, we help our clients build learning ecosystems that are:

- adaptable to change  
- auditable and secure  
- maintainable over time  
- aligned with public-sector and enterprise standards  

Whenever possible, we contribute back to the ecosystems we rely on, sharing tools, extensions, and best practices developed through real-world projects.
